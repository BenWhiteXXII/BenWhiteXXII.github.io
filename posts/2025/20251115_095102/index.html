American Style Pancakes

Posted on November 15, 2025 in recipe

A classic pancake recipe.

Ingredients

  • 135g plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar
  • 130ml milk
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • maple syrup + bacon (optional)

Method

  1. Combine baking powder, flour, salt and sugar into a bowl.
  2. Whisk together the milk and egg, then the melted butter.
  3. Pour into the flour mixture and lightly beat, leaving a few small lumps of flour.
  4. Heat up a frying pan and add a little butter.
  5. Spoon in the pancake batter.
  6. When the pancake begins to bubble, flip and check underside.
  7. Repeat and top with maple syrup and bacon
