A Note on Comic Books in My Collection

2024-01-10

Over the past few days I’ve taken to reading comic books, specifically Hellblazer and Hellboy.

Since most comic books are published on a per issue basis, I was worried that my digital book collection would quickly become unreadable with the amount of issues that would pile up. For reference, my current system works like this:

Book Title (Series name + place in series) - Author Name

However, for comic books it’ll look like this:

Series Title #Issue no. - Author Name

That should tidy things up a bit. I mainly made this update so that I could add something to my blog for once, I’ve been feeling lately that I’m sleeping on its potential. I’ll try to upload a small blog post once a week going forward - like a book or movie review, or just a discussion about something on the web.

That’s it for now, happy new year!

ADDENDUM - 18/01/24

This post has become pretty redundant since I switched to reading omnibuses for my comic fix (better on the bank account). I’ll still keep this small post up for the time being, just in case I do read any individual issues in the future.
