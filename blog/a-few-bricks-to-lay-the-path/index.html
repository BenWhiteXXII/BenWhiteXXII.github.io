A Few Bricks to Lay the Path

2023-06-26

First things first. Let’s lay down some rules.

  1. Being prolific is the first rule. I’m going to aim to write a post at least once a day to keep the muscles flexible. Now the great thing about this rule is that I don’t have to post what I write but it’ll put some pressure on me to keep my brain active.
  2. Just because a post is posted doesn’t mean you can’t repost. Go back to things, play with similar ideas and see if you’ve gotten anywhere .
  3. Listen to other people. Read other people’s stuff. Steal Borrow what you can. There will always be someone else with a better idea. This is an encouraging thought.
  4. Keep on experimenting. Don’t just sit in your lane, go out of your way to try new things and experiences

This list will probably grow over time so don’t be shocked if it shows up again in your rss feed.
