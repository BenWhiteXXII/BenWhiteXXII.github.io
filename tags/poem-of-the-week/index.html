Posted on March 8, 2024

Prayer

I ask you this:
Which way to go?
I ask you this:
Which sin to bear?
Which crown to put
Upon my hair?
I do not know,
Lord God,
I do not know.
– Langston Hughes, Selected Poems (2020), publ. Serpent’s Tail
